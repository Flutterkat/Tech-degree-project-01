My Hobbies

Dabbling In:

Computers: I have been playing with computers for many years in my free time, fixing both hardware and software issues for family and friends. I even built my own rig in 2020.

SCUBA Diving: I've always had a deeply ingrained passion for the water and fish. I started off fishing at a young age and eventually decided to take a shark fishing trip out of Florida in my early 20's. That trip sparked my interest in SCUBA Diving and i set out a year later to attain my SCUBA certifications. I advanced my SCUBA skills till I obtained my Advanced Open-water Dive certification and Nitrox certification so i can take longer dives at deeper depths. To date my deepest dive has been 90 feet on the the wreck of the Mercedes, a sunken ship off the coast of Florida.
